Olivia Dean
Accueil

Derrière chaque paroles et chaque chanson, un vers futile.

Découvre ma poésie dissimulée...

A Flower to seduce me,
A Flower to charm me,
A Flower to make me smile.
Eros knows me well,
I trust him only to fall in love.

It's my own soul, my own
company;
When you complain that
I am messy,
I find myself rather
lovely.
I am my own harmony.

The glow of your eyes
Reflects the beauty I’ve
always held within.
You make me feel like a
shining star,
And I dive in once more.

Apart from reality,
Was it 'cause of vanity?
Now these minutes belong to us,
So let's not make a fuss.

Always the same story,
Under the Italian sky, we try.
No one wants to live a misery,
We try, we fall, we rise, we try.

Where to land this UFO?
I don't know what I'm doin,
Should I look below?
I don't know what I'm doin,
Should I gently fly down nice
and slow?
